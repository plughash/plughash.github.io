About plug#

plug# is commonly known as a successor for Origem Woot, brand new, open source, fresh start with many more improved features!

Features

We're still on Alpha but working hard! Currently we have the main features you need, AutoWoot, AutoJoin, AutoGrab, we even have a Reverse Woot feature that disguises your auto voting! If you have any feedback be sure to contact us!

Emotes

All the emotes from Origem Woot are available on plug#, aswell as the whole Twitch.tv collection, including sub emotes! If you want to contribute just let us know on plug.dj/plughash!
