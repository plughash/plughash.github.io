Core Features

We still have a lot to do but so far we've gotten you th basics! You currently have access to AutoWoot, AutoJoin, AutoGrab and even a Reverse Woot feature that disguises your votes! Keep in touch and you'll see more features in the near future.

Private Messaging

We're still on Alpha but working hard! PMing is one of the most demanded things from Origem and many people are looking forward to it on plug#, it will still take us a while as we're reworking it and creating new and fun ways to use it. Stay tuned.

Customization

With plug# you have the freedom to turn plug into your own place! If you are a room owner you can make or request a theme for your room that all the plug# users in your room will see! If you don't host a room you can still use our pre-set themes!
